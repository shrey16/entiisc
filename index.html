EntIISc

Entrepreneurship & Innovation @IISc

Vision

An international hub for Entrepreneurship and Innovation

Objectives

Promote and support entrepreneurship and innovation activities

Mission

Facilitate ideas and networking.

EntIISc is a voluntary student body that spearheads student entrepreneurship and innovation efforts at the Indian Institute of Science. Led by an interdisciplinary team, the student body receives support from professors on campus, industry veterans and stakeholders in entrepreneurial ecosystem. The student body also works closely with IISc based incubator Society for Innovation and Development (SID).

  • Reach

    Reached more than 1500 Students and Alumni across the city.

  • Collaboration

    Collaborated with multiple start-ups, incubators and institutions.

  • Student Support

    Connected 4 student teams with Angel networks, IAN and SiD

  • Start-Up

    Supported 4 student entrepreneurs to start their own companies.
